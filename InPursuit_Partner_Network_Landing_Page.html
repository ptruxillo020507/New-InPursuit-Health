Neural Wearable Technology

Pison Technology

pison.com →

Pison's AI-powered neural sensors capture neurological and cognitive performance data from a wrist-worn wearable — developed with the NIH, DoD, and NSF. Their CARE platform enables early detection and remote monitoring of neurological disorders, while PERFORM tracks mental agility, focus, and readiness for athletes and workers.

What They're Building on TETRA Pison is integrating their neural biometric data streams into THP's data fabric — giving their customers a unified health profile that combines cognitive performance with clinical records, wearable data, and wellness metrics. Through the InPursuit Marketplace, Pison's technology reaches providers, employers, and consumers already on the platform.
Care Coordination & PHR

Coordinated Services of America

coordinatedservicesofamerica.com →

CSA's MyDocs™ platform creates patient-owned portable health records, enabling consumers to upload, consolidate, and share medical documents with their care team. Their solution addresses the core problem of fragmented physician-to-physician and physician-to-patient communication with HIPAA-compliant document management, telemedicine integration, and caregiver access controls.

What They're Building on TETRA CSA is connecting their MyDocs™ portable health records into THP's data fabric — giving patients a single place to manage documents, share records with providers, and access AI-powered health insights. Their ACO leadership team brings decades of care coordination expertise and a direct pipeline to provider networks and Medicare populations.
