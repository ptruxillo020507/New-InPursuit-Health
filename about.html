Vision Statement
We are InPursuit of the idea, promise, and opportunity that our health data will drive immediate and long-term personalized treatment and wellness options through a more engaged, connected, efficient, proactive, and Person-Centric Healthcare Ecosystem.
Mission Statement
InPursuit Health, LLC is committed to revolutionizing healthcare by harnessing the TETRA™ Healthcare Platform to integrate diverse health data, delivering AI-powered insights and innovative solutions that empower individuals, providers, payers, employers, and pharmacies. Through a parallel approach, we’ve built a collaborative, interoperable ecosystem that drives efficiency, reduces costs, and fosters a proactive, person-centric approach to wellness.

Our Story

From the battlefield
to the healthcare frontier.

InPursuit Health was founded by veterans and data industry pioneers who have operated in the most demanding, security-sensitive environments on earth — including the U.S. Department of Defense, the Department of Veterans Affairs, and over 400 law enforcement agencies.

Our leadership team has been building enterprise data architecture since the 1990s, working across 30+ national governments and the world’s largest data providers. When we looked at American healthcare, we saw the same broken silos, buried data, and friction that we’d spent careers solving in defense and intelligence.

So we built TETRA™ — the healthcare data fabric that connects providers, payers, consumers, and pharmacies into one interoperable ecosystem. Not another layer on top of broken systems, but a new foundation underneath them.

Today, InPursuit Health is a recognized member of the CMS Health Tech Ecosystem, selected alongside companies like Apple, Google, Amazon, and Oracle. But unlike those companies, we didn’t build the silos. We built the bridge.
