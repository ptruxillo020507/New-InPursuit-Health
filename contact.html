Charlotte, NC — the emerging

Charlotte isn’t just a banking powerhouse anymore. The city has emerged as one of America’s fastest-growing health technology hubs, anchored by the Pearl District — a purpose-built innovation campus designed to incubate the next generation of healthcare companies.

With world-class health systems like Atrium Health and Novant Health headquartered here, a growing ecosystem of healthtech startups, and proximity to the Research Triangle, Charlotte offers the perfect intersection of healthcare expertise, financial infrastructure, and entrepreneurial energy.

InPursuit Health chose Charlotte not just because of where the city is today — but because of where it’s going. The same ambition that drives our platform drives our community.
